Company profile questions answered

What is the Company Directory?

A Company Profile allows your aviation brand to have a presence on Aviation Job Search whether you’re recruiting or not. Whether you just want to include a basic overview, such as head office location or company size, or a fully branded page with a cover image and video, your Company Profile page is the perfect place to do this – and for free.

What does a Company Profile include?

Creating a company profile is free and includes space for you to include a basic overview (company type, sectors, size, company mission etc), a more in-depth overview and any branding and multimedia to help promote your company as a great place to work (for example, branded cover image or embedded video). You can also include a feed of jobs that candidates can opt into receiving alerts for.

You’ll also be listed in our Company Directory. Many job seekers seek to work for a particular company, so being able to find your company listed here gives your brand (and jobs) more exposure.

Why should I have a Company Profile?

More than ever candidates and jobseekers want to find out who they’ll be working for as much as what they’ll be doing. It also keeps your company front-of-mind for people, even when they’re not actively searching for jobs. It’s free, and having a free company profile could be the difference between someone applying for your vacancies or not.

Can I change it in the future?

Yes, it 's easy. Simply log in and change anything you need to. Like a social media profile, you simply edit, save and preview all at the same time.

Why should I use Aviation Job Search?

Aviation is a lifestyle industry and many job seekers aspire to work for specific airlines or companies. It 's important to be listed with the other airlines on our company directory so you don 't miss out to other recruiting companies.
