Questions and answers

What is CV Discovery?

CV Discovery lets you browse, read and save the aviation profiles and CVs of over 140,000 aviation professionals absolutely free. By purchasing download credits, you get enhanced access and can also download their full CVs. This gives you first mover advantage when it comes to headhunting quality people.

What does CV Discovery include?

It’s absolutely free to search, filter and read over 140,000 aviation profiles and CVs. This gives you access to the candidate’s skills, professional experience, location and education. You can save candidates of interest and even privately rate and add notes. With download credits, you additionally get access to their contact details and full CV.

How does CV Discovery work?

Simply create an account for free (with no credit card required) and start browsing – it really is as simple as that.

Why should I use CV Discovery?

It’s free. You aren’t wasting download credits on candidates who aren’t suitable for the roles you’re looking to fill. Our candidates are also relevant; they’re in the aviation industry and you can filter out those who haven’t updated for a while or with incomplete profiles.

Why should I use you for downloading CVs?

We love aviation. Our candidates love aviation. Our combined passion means you get access to relevant candidates. Also, not only is our audience niche, but our product is too, and we give you useful filters (like aircraft make and model, flight hours…) to help you narrow down your search and simplify aviation hiring.
