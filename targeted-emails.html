Frequently asked questions

What are Targeted Emails?

Targeted Emails allow you to send your content to our email subscribers. The messaging is up to you; promote vacancies or simply show our subscribers why your airline or company is a great place to work. Our mobile-first templates give you the flexibility to include images, text and even jobs that link directly to your vacancies.

How do Targeted Emails work?

You and your account manager will work together to choose the best audience and the best send time and then we can create your email. You can either choose the modules and layout that best suits your campaign, or you can simply send over the content and leave it to us. Afterwards, we’ll make sure you see how many opens and clicks your email got.

Why should I use Targeted Emails?

This is an incredibly flexible product where you get control over who sees your email and what your email includes. It can include your branding and arrives directly into the inboxes of aviation professionals who have specifically signed up to see emails like yours.

Why should I use you for Targeted Emails?

Our audience. We have 113,000 active subscribers and they are aviation professionals who are looking for the next move. Not only this, but you can further narrow down the audience depending on their role, location or when they signed up.
