Your questions answered

What is Post a Job?

Post a Job is the fastest way to get your vacancies in front of an engaged audience of aviation job seekers. Our Post a Job system is fully self service allowing you to place an ad quickly, while it also talks you through the process giving you a live preview so that you can get the best response. Once live, candidates can apply directly through the job ad, and you can manage them through our candidate tracking system.

What does Post a Job include?

Post a Job includes access to our fast and simple ad builder so that you can advertise the role, and also include information about your company. You also get access to our candidate tracking system so you can easily manage any applications. We also give you access to full reporting showing how your advert is doing, even benchmarking it against similar roles from other recruiters.

How does Post a Job work?

Simply go to www.aviationjobsearch.com and click Post a Job – the process is fast and simple. Once placed, you can access reporting and applications via your recruiter dashboard, and you also get access to an account manager who will help you get the most out of your aviation recruitment campaign.

Why should I use Post a Job?

If you have a vacancy to fill, posting a job is the best way to get it seen. Our website is a destination for aviation jobseekers who search specifically for jobs like yours. It also makes it easy to manage applications and gives you access to optional extras such as Response Boosters which can help you fill hard to fill roles. Airlines hiring candidates can get access to a talented pool of aviation professionals.

How much does Post a Job cost?

We’re completely transparent about pricing and our pricing calculator will show you the price you’ll pay before you even start creating your ad. The more you post, the less it costs, so the most you will pay will be £699 for a single advert, but this quickly goes down and you can save over 50% by posting 10 adverts.

Why should I use you for aviation job advertising?

We’re a niche website with a niche audience. This means that you will get more relevant applications from exactly the kind of people you want to hear from. We’ve been in the aviation industry for over 20 years now, so we know what we’re doing. We also work with multiposters, so posting a job fits perfectly within your existing workflow.
