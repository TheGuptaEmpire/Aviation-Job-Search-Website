Answers to your most popular questions

What is Category sponsorship.html?

Category sponsorship.html is one of our most impactful products and gets you and your brand in front of a huge audience, both on our website and in the emails we send. Your branding will appear throughout your chosen category, both on and off site.

What does Category sponsorship.html include?

By sponsoring a category, your brand appears front and centre – and top and bottom – you get a branded banner at the top of every category page seamlessly integrating your jobs and company profile page, animated web banners throughout job listings and you also appear in job by email alerts as well. You’ll become synonymous with that brand.

Why should I use Category sponsorship.html?

Exposure, and lots of it. You will become a byword for that category and will be unmissable with your brand appearing on all touchpoints. Each category gets thousands of page views, hundreds of applications and appears in tens of thousands of emails every month – and so too can your brand.

How much does Category sponsorship.html cost?

We have different bands of categories taking into account the amount of exposure you will get. Pricing starts from £199 monthly for some of our smaller, more-niche categories, up to £1500 for the categories that get the most views, have the highest ranking, most applications and so on.

How does Category sponsorship.html work?

Choose the category that best suits you, and leave the rest to us. If you need pilots, choose pilots. If you specialise in aviation engineering, choose Engineers. Our prices vary but they 're proportional to the response you 'll get. You can supply banners, or we can create them for you and incorporate into our site. We recommend making sure your company profile is up to date and you have jobs listed to get the most out of your sponsorship.html.

Why should I use Aviation Job Search?

We started as a jobs board but now have a portfolio of tools to help aviation recruiters find great people. Our market is your market, and our candidates are exactly who you’re looking for.
