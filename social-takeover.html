Your questions answered

What is Social Takeover?

It’s a fully managed, 24 hour campaign where you can promote your brand on our marketing channels to a combined audience of 420,000. This product is perfect for people who want to promote their workplace culture or promote hard to fill vacancies through social posts, stories, emails and web banners.

What does a Social Takeover include?

You can choose the products that best suit your campaign. We will promote, post and follow up with a fully transparent report. We can even help with the design. Choose any of the following products:
‍
Promotional posts on our social media prior to the takeover
Carousel posts on our social media accounts, including video
Go live or post stories to Instagram and Facebook
Email our subscribers with a targeted email
Takeover our website with animated display banners

Additionally, we can help you create content for an additional fee. We can either come on site and film, photograph and optimise your takeover, or our off-site package means you can tell us what you need, and our design team will create the content for you.

How does a Social Takeover work?

We will first have an onboarding call where you can brief us. You can then send through your content – we can help with the design, before making sure it is scheduled for the agreed date. Following the campaign, we will give you a full, detailed report.

I don 't have time to create content. Can you help?

We’re marketing experts and can help you create your takeover. For an additional fee, we can either come on site to help film, photograph and discuss your takeover, or our off-site package means we can create the takeover designs for you based on your requirements.

Why should I have a Social Takeover?

70% of people are passive jobseekers – they’re looking for a job but they just don’t know it. Our social takeovers are a great opportunity to engage with these people and show off why your airline or company is a great place to work. Our audience is also extremely niche – so not only do you reach lots of people, but you reach the right people too.

Why should I use you for my Social Takeover?

This is an innovative product not available anywhere else. We are aviation-specific and you get direct access to our huge audience which means it’s also a relevant audience.
