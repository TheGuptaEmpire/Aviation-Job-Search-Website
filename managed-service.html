Your questions answered

What is Managed Service?

You can either boost ads from your recruiter dashboard, or create an account for free to start creating a new ad to boost.If you need help or advice, give our account managers a call today on 01772 639605

What does Managed Service include?

Everything. The single flat fee includes a briefing meeting with your account, advertising for the role, searching and headhunting, screening and shortlisting, access to our applicant tracking system, full reporting and as many hires as you wish to make.

How does Managed Service work?

It starts with a call with your account manager who will work to understand your exact requirements. You then leave the rest to us as we will promote the role and headhunt for suitable candidates. We’re fully transparent so you can see exactly how we whittle down the list of candidates to just a few quality prospects and will supply you with advanced profiles for each. Then it’s back to you – all you need to do is interview, and then you can make as many hires as you wish for no extra fee.

Why should I use Managed Service?

This is the fast, economical way to recruit the right people. The cost per hire is minimised because we do the hard work for you and go to extraordinary lengths to find the right candidates. You also don’t pay per hire – this means the price you pay up front is the only price you pay meaning the cost per hire gets lower, not higher, with the more people you recruit.

Why should I use you for my managed recruitment?

We live and breathe aviation. We have priority access to airline industry databases and job boards that generic recruitment agencies simply don’t. Our flat fee model can also save recruiters hundreds, if not thousands, and you can also relax in the knowledge we’re delivering quality prospects.
