FAQs

What is sponsorship.html?

Every year we create reports and guides that recruiters and jobseekers can download and keep. Airlines and companies can sponsor these reports and guides and get seen by an audience of thousands. We offer different packages, including the ability to include your logo and short company bio, all the way up to title sponsorship.html which includes your company brand on every available page.

What does sponsorship.html include?

We have different levels to suit different budgets. Choose:

Supporting Sponsor: Includes logo and 60 word company bio within our guides and reports

Title Sponsor: As above, plus logo and brand name on front page, your brand name on every available page, your logo and brand name in supporting marketing material plus full page editorial. You also appear in ‘The Check In’, a retrospective of the last 12 months in an interactive, sharable website at the last 12 months

What is 'The Check In '?

The Check In is our new interactive minisite which summarises the last 12 months in an engaging, sharable way with versions for both job seekers and recruiters. It is fully mobile responsive and will be heavily promoted on all our platforms. Your logos will appear on the first and last page of The Check In, along with the downloadable version of our annual guide.

How does sponsorship.html work?

When you speak to our account manager we will help you choose the best solution for your objectives. You will get to see any designs prior to publication and then your brand will be on every download the report or guide gets.

Why should I use sponsorship.html?

sponsorship.html gives you evergreen exposure to a relevant audience. Our different sponsorship.html levels let you choose the option best for your brand and your budget. Also, ‘The Check In’ minisite is totally unique to Aviation Job Search.

Why should I use you for sponsorship.html?

Aviation is what we do. Our guides help recruiters understand what’s happening in the industry and help jobseekers find their next challenge. There are few places that afford you such direct access to such a niche audience.
