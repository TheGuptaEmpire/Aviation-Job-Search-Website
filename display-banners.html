Questions? We have answers

What are Display Banners?

Display banners, also known as web banners or banner ads, appear throughout our job search and Career Hub websites and are fully branded, animated and are a great way to get your brand seen. They are clickable and can link directly to wherever you want them to and are fully mobile responsive so appear on desktop and mobile.

What do Display Banners include?

Display Banners appear in a variety of standard sizes throughout our websites including leaderboard, mobile leaderboard and MPU. You can choose where your banners appear; site wide, in a particular section or even just on our Career Hub and you can create ads that display your messaging and branding as you want.

How do Display Banners work?

You first work with our account manager to decide how you want your campaign to run and where you want it to appear, and then you can either send over your creatives or leave it to us to create the designs for you. We’ll also make sure you get a report showing how many people saw and clicked on your ads.

Why should I use Web Banners?

These compliment the job ads and article on our site perfectly and appeal to an already engaged niche audience. They can stand out by using your branding and be fully animated. They are perfect to run as part of a recruitment campaign or to attract interest in hard-to-fill vacancies.

Why should I use you for banner advertising?

We’re niche, and we can segment our audience further too. It means that any response is a relevant response. If you are looking to fill an aviation vacancy or simply want aviation professionals to discover your airline or company, Aviation Job Search is the perfect platform for you.
